# Privacy and Compliance

## App Store Privacy Readiness
Before production submit:
- Confirm data collection categories are documented.
- Confirm tracking behavior is declared correctly.
- Confirm third-party SDK data handling is reviewed.
- Confirm privacy policy URL is available.

## Generated Checklist Artifact
When `WithPrivacyChecklist` is enabled, the project must include:
- `docs/privacy-checklist.md`

## Human Gate Reminder
Privacy declarations and legal text are human-owned dependencies and must be listed as unresolved when missing.
